    • Yes. Air Jordan or Jordan is a renowned athletic sportswear brand under the parent company Nike. It was the designers at Nike that designed the first-ever Air Jordan Shoes for the famous basketball player Micheal Jordan in early 1986. By 1997, the Jordan franchise had grown to such a significant degree that Nike decided to make it a stand-alone brand.

  • Does Nike shoes make formal shoes?

    • No. Nike does not produce any formal shoes. However, Nike does make leather shoes that can be sported to formal events.

    • Here are some of the key differences between Nike and Jordan Shoes
  • Customer Appeal: Nike is a pure athletic sportswear brand that makes shoes for all sorts of purposes Jordan on the other hand, focuses mainly on producing shoes for the purposes of playing basketball and looking stylish.

Though the retail price of the jordans is affordable, their resale values can double its retail price and even more. This makes it one of the most expensive shoes one can find in the market.
