        Types of Protein Powder

        Whey supplement is highly popular with the community of bodybuilders, fitness enthusiasts, and athletes. It is obtained from milk during the cheese-making process. The watery portion that is present on the top of curd is whey. It goes through rigorous filtration processes to be obtained in a powdered form that is sold in the market as whey protein for men and women.

        This protein powder contains all the 9 essential amino acids that help in muscle growth and recovery. There are mainly three types of whey supplement - whey protein isolate, whey protein concentrate, and whey protein hydrolysate.

        1. Whey Concentrate: It contains 60-80% protein and the rest is carbs and fats. It tastes great and is ideal for people who are into fitness and wish to build great muscles. Whey protein concentrate helps in building muscle mass and enhances muscle recovery.
        2. Whey Isolate: Whey is further processed to be transformed into whey protein isolate. It has around 90-95% protein with a low level of fats and carbs. Isolate is comparatively lower in lactose content which makes it ideal for lactose-intolerant people. It tastes smooth and helps with muscle growth. 
        3. Whey Hydrolyzed: Hydrolyzed Whey is pre-digested which allows better absorption and digestion. It is an expensive version of protein.

        So, these were the main whey protein types, apart from this plant protein can also help you to achieve your body goals. If you are someone who is lactose intolerant or a vegan, plant based protein is the right pick for you.
